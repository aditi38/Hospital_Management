Specialties And Super Specialties



Centres of Excellence

LifeCare has dedicated Centers of Excellence for several key specialties and super specialties. They are unique and state-of-the-art facilities spread across several of the LifeCare Hospital location and each Centre of Excellence stands out as a citadel of excellence.

At LifeCare, we consider it our prime responsibility to provide high-quality clinical care to all our patients. Putting the patient at the core of our operations, we have developed robust quality standards, used expert diagnoses and treatment plans and enhanced infection, and safety protocols to render them the most appropriate treatment.

Top specilities

Cardiologist

The LifeCare are regarded as one of the best heart hospitals in India, performing a multitude of treatments and procedures in cardiology and cardiothoracic surgery. The scorecard shows an unmatched record of over 1,52,000 cardiac and cardiothoracic surgeries. Our team of cardiologists and cardiothoracic surgeons are trained at the top institutes in India and abroad and are completely dedicated to the treatment for coronary heart diseases.

Neurosurgeon

The LifeCare of Neurosciences is regarded as one of the best neurology and neurosurgery hospital in India with a legacy of innovation and excellence. Ably assisted by the latest Neuro-Radiology services, Neuro-Intensive Care facilities and Medical and Radiation Oncology services, our Neurologists and Neurosurgeons achieve outcomes in neurological disease & treatments matching those of the leading institutions across the globe

Orthopaedic Surgeon

LifeCare Hospitals offers key & best orthopedic surgeries and procedures in India. We perform bone and joint replacement surgeries which include the most current Arthroscopic and Reconstructive techniques. Joint replacements including hip resurfacing and knee replacement surgery [primary, complex primary & revision replacements] are being done in large numbers with excellent outcomes

Oncologist

Cancer of any stage can be ruthless and challenging to treat. The oncology department of the LifeCare Hospital offers an extensive range of full-fledged treatments in the diagnosis and treatment of all types of cancer. The treatment aims in providing a holistic coverage of cancer treatment that includes active management of all types of cancer through surgeries, chemotherapies, and regular monitoring and counseling sessions.

Gastroenterology

The Institutes of Gastroenterology are regarded as one of the best & top gastroenterology hospital in India. We are dedicated to the management of diseases of the digestive and hepatobiliary systems in children and adults. The gastroenterology surgeons offers expert care in both Medical and Surgical Gastroenterology. The Institutes possess modern state of art equipment backed by advanced intensive care units

ENT

Ear, nose and throat (ENT) specialist is also known as otolaryngologists. This department deals with medical issues related to balance and hearing, sleeping and breathing issues, speech and swallowing, neck and head cancer, sinuses and allergies, skin disorders and facial plastic surgery. The ent department of LifeCare Hospital is highly equipped with facilities to provide treatments for issues related to the ear, nose and throat.

Dentist

Dentistry Department at LifeCare takes coordinate care of both children and adults. The professionals look into the patient’s needs and condition to provide multidisciplinary care. We have a team of licensed dental specialists who provide routine care and special practices to ensure treatment for every type of dental problem. In addition to this, our specialists help the patient manage problems relating to dental hygiene, etc. Moreover, our dentist also offers titanium-tipped ultrasonic painless scalers and a mouth spa for preventing gum infection and decay of teeth

Psychiatrist

The Department of Behavioral Science and mental health of LifeCare contains a team of highly experienced professionals working in this field for a long time .Our Department specializes in mental health problems, including mood disorders, depression, anxiety problems, sleep difficulty, etc., and suggests treatment accordingly. We focus on psychological well-being, emotional satisfaction and occupational functioning. Apart from this, we also educate our patients about managing relationships, stressful situations and the ability to make decisions for improving effects of their mental health.

Gynecologist

LifeCare has an extensive Obgyn and IVF department that promotes a “one-stop solution” to all the problems women face. Our IVF facility opts for the best therapeutical route for women bearing issues of adulthood till menopause and even after that. Our OB-GYN specialists, medical surgeons, and embryologists work with other departments to offer the best treatment plan. Our experts conduct numerous examinations to ensure an accurate diagnosis. They have expertise in infertility, pelvic pain, high-risk pregnancy, menopause, etc.
